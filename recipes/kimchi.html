Kimchi Fried Rice

Whats for dinner?

Kimchi fried rice (Kimchi Bokkeumbap,) is a type of Korean fried rice and it is predominantly cooked with kimchi (obviously!) To give it more texture and flavor a variety of meat and/or vegetables are often added.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Kimchi
  • 5 oz bacon, cut small
  • 3 cups white rice
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 1/2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp cooiking oil

Steps

  1. First, On medium high heat preheat a pan/wok and once heated, add the cooking oil and spread it well with a spatula.
  2. Next, Add the garlic, stir it fast for about 10 seconds. Then add the bacon and stir it well until half of it is cooked.
  3. After, Add the Kimchi and stir until 80% of it is cooked.
  4. Then, Add the rice and the kimchi juice. Mix all of them together well and thoroughly.
  5. Finally, Add the sesame oil and mix them well. Remove from the heat.
  6. At Last, Serve the Kimchi fried rice on a plate. Garnish with the sesame seeds, green onion and seaweed strips. (Garnish is all optional). Place the cooked egg on top. Enjoy!
